ABOUT

CG Designs is a design studio specialized in creating a unique atmosphere for living and working spaces, as well as constructing amazing interior designs that capture the mood and taste of its customers. From bespoke furniture designs to incredible upholstery pieces made by the fines sewing production, the CG design studio has its own bespoke collection to embellish its amazing design projects.
